welcome

Colors Of Favela is a project that is committed to revitalizing homes in one of the biggest favelas in Brazil - Paraisópolis (São Paulo) through a collection of NFT’s inspired on the community and their local artists.

The revitalization of this area will include plastering and painting the brick houses with vibrant and joyful colors. Based on research made in different countries, it was identified that this type of initiative helps decrease crime rates and increase resident’s sense of belonging, they also feel pride to be part of the slum, and they feel more confident to be part of this community.

DAB has joined the G10 Favelas, a group of Social Impact Leaders and Entrepreneurs from the 10 largest Favelas in Brazil. Hands of Maria, which allows delivery of thousands lunch boxes to those who have experienced the cruel reality of being in hunger vulnerability. FAQ

What are Colors of Favela?

Colors Of Favela is a Brazilian NFT project that is committed to revitalizing homes in one of the biggest favelas in Brazil - Paraisópolis (São Paulo) through a collection of NFT’s inspired on the community and their local artists.

The revitalization of this area will include plastering and painting the brick houses with vibrant and joyful colors. Based on research made in different countries, it was identified that this type of initiative helps decrease crime rates and increase resident’s sense of belonging, they also feel pride to be part of the slum, and they feel more confident to be part of this community.

What is the concept behind our NFT?

We chose to represent a house in the NFTs, because a home is a symbol, show where we live, mark our achievements, being a person's safe place to be. No matter where a house is, alone in the forest or inside big cities, they protect and shelter us. With this in mind, the arts are inspired by the houses that were built in Paraisópolis, through a lot of work, sweat and with the possibilities of each person inside the slum, showing the diversity of those constructions, such as doors, windows, paintings and other elements that will be added with each NFT acquired. With this idea, the NFT will talk within the metaverse and show the world how favelas are like in Brazil, representing a place and an identity of belonging.

What is the social impact the project is expecting?

During the Project we will make different initiatives to support Paraisópolis community, also including initiatives based on NFT’s holders’ interests.

We’ll perform seminars to teach about Play to Earn games, Cryptos, NFTs, technologies, art-recycling (ministered by Berbela, a local plastic artist from Paraisópolis), also revenue a community park area and produce a big art event dedicated to those people and revitalize more than 100 houses.

For the owners of NFT's collections, we will offer raffles with unique arts, products in partnership with Costurando Sonhos, also from other highly performance NFT’s projects, pre-launch purchase opportunities based on new collections, and many other initiatives.
